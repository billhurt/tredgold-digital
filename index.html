Where friction costs you customers

Every lost customer left at a point of friction.

The problem is rarely the product — it's the journey around it. I find where customers are dropping off or not coming back, and fix those points end to end.

01

First impression & conversion

A website that doesn't clearly explain what you do, or an enquiry form that takes too long to complete, kills potential work before you even know about it. I build sites and landing pages designed around getting that first response.

02

Booking, payment & onboarding

If getting from "interested" to "confirmed" involves more than one or two steps, you'll lose people — especially the ones who have options. I streamline booking flows, payment, and onboarding so customers get through cleanly and you spend less time chasing.

03

Ongoing experience & self-service

The best clients don't want to wait on hold or dig through email threads to get an answer. SaaS tools, portals and AI-powered search give them what they need instantly — and reduce the support overhead on your side.

04

Repeat business & retention

Winning a customer once is the expensive part. I build systems that keep them engaged and make it easy to come back — whether that's automated communication, renewal flows, or SEO and content that keeps you front of mind.

Why this matters for retention, not just acquisition

Most agencies only care about getting you new customers.

Getting new customers is expensive. Losing good ones — because the booking process was awkward, the follow-up was slow, or the self-service experience was poor — is expensive in a different way. I work on both sides of that: making it easier to convert the first time, and making the ongoing experience good enough that there's no reason to go elsewhere.
