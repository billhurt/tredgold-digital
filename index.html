Common questions about small business automation

What is automation for small businesses?

It means using software to handle repetitive tasks automatically — like responding to missed calls, confirming bookings, collecting deposits, and following up with leads — without doing it manually.

Most systems are live within 7 days. We handle all the setup, configuration, and testing — no technical knowledge is needed on your end.

We work with small service businesses across the UK — car detailers, cleaners, gardeners, and tradespeople who rely on fast response times to win work.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes — all packages are designed to grow with your business. Start with missed call recovery and add booking, deposits, and full automation at any time.
